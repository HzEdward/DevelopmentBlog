About the Project

The Red Cross Red Crescent runs community surveys in nearly all their programming. It is critical for understanding the views of affected populations, and how effective programmes are. Often times, country-level teams do not have technical expertise in sampling. Existing free tools are either overly simple (basic calculators that do not take into consideration methodology) or are very complex (requiring high-level knowledge of sampling statistics)
